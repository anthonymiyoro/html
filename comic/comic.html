Thuon-D

Hekaya za Mashujaa

Finally, for the first time ever Kenya is about to have its very own manga series.'Thoun D' is an exciting action packed, fantasy adventure manga created by Wuon Onyalo, a Kenyan mangaka.It tells the story of a struggle for peace between to between the six major tribes that has been heavily involved in all the previous wars.
The story begins in ziwani, one of the six major tribes that has been heavily involved in all the previous wars.The series is in its late stages of production and its first chapter is soon to be released. Created by Wuon Onayalo, the series will be published on a weekly schedule by 360 degrees communications limited and is expected to be available in newstands all over the country.Each comic will be 6-10 pages long and published in black and white retailing at 100 shillings. Stay tuned for more details in regards to the comic.

Story

It has been many years since the end of the first Great Spirit War, but the world has never known true peace.The six major tribes of the world; Ziwani, Peponi, Ardhini, Mtoni, Msituni and Mlimani have continued to wage wars for power and dominance. Fed up with the endless death and destruction, Nyame the 'Ruoth' of Ziwani unleashed a frightening power that put an end to the violence. Even then, true peace has remained elusive and the deadly wars have left in their place an entity with the power to either save the world or destroy it forever...

About the Creator

Wuon Onyalo is a Nairobi-born mangaka and the creator of Thuon-D:Hekaya za Mashujaa.When he isnt working on Thuon-D:Hekaya za Mashujaa he spends his time learning Japanese, making music and, of course, reading manga and watching anime. He started work on Thuon-D:Hekaya za Mashujaa over 5 years ago.
